My Hobbies

Baking

One of the things I most love is to bake, especially desserts. Below you can find some of my favourite traditional portuguese desserts.

Natas do Céu

This is another typical portuguese dessert, which translates to "Camel Drool". Don't worry, it doesn't have drool in it! It has a weird name but it is delicious.

Ingredients:Heavy Cream, Crushed Cookie, Egg Custard

Baba de Camelo

This is a typical portuguese dessert, which translates to "Cream of Heaven"

Ingredients:Cooked Condensed Milk, Egg whites (It's that simple!)

Pão de Ló de Ovar

This is a typical portuguese dessert, which translates to "Ló Bread"It is a cake from the city Ovar, made with a huge amount of eggs, just how the portuguese like it!

Ingredients:11 yolks, 2 whole eggs, Sugar, Flour.
